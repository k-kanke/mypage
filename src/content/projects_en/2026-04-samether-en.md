Built Samether.io, a real-time multiplayer browser game for PC and mobile. Players control sharks and grow in-game based on their real-world movement, while competing with others through territorial battles and predation. The concept was inspired by `Slither.io`.

The project won the Grand Prize at the Progate Hackathon powered by AWS.
