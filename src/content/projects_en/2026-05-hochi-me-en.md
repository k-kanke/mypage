HochiMe is a web application where an AI clone reflecting the user's preferences autonomously explores a 3D virtual world and brings back interests and possibilities the user has not yet noticed.

The one-line pitch was: "While you leave it alone, another you discovers unknown passions." The product was built at Engineer Guild Hackathon 2026 presented by Mercari, hosted by Gaishishukatsu.com.

The main tech stack included Next.js, React, TypeScript, Three.js, Supabase, and Google Gemini API.

The project won Engineer Guild Hackathon 2026 presented by Mercari.
